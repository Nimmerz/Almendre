Business Description

We are the Stevia people. We pioneered the purification of the Stevia leaf and its natural extracts. Almendra offers the most consistent and cleanest tasting range of stevia-based extracts available today.

Mission

We are the Stevia people.By using the latest thinking in the agronomy,extraction and purification of Stevia we will bring natural sweetening solutions to the worlds food and beverage consumers

History

The roots of Almendra go back to 2002 when Stevian Biotechnology Corporation was founded in Malaysia. The company grew, listed on the AIM stock exchange in 2007 and was renamed as Pure Circle Ltd. The founding family exited the Company and created Almendra Pte Ltd in 2009 in Singapore with a plant in Thailand.
